20 Questions to Get to Know Me

  1. What’s your philosophy in life?
    2. The Golden Rule: treat others the way you want to be treated. And also, encourage health in all aspects of life: mental and emotional health, physical health, spiritual health, financial health, etc. There is room for improvement for everyone!
  2. What habit do you have that you think not many other people have?
    3. I love organizing and tracking things! I have a spreadsheet consisting of all sorts of things (including daily goals and habits), and now I also have a website to organize things into.
  3. How would your friends describe you?
    • My best friend said "[f]riendly, methodical, determined, intelligent, and... musical."
    • Friend #2 said "funny, smart, warm, creative, talented, [and] quirky."
    • Friend #3 said "[k]ind, neurotic, analytical, curious, and last but not least nice smelling."
  4. What are your values?
    5. Hard work, building up talent, and organization should be prioritized.
  5. Is it hard for you to accept feedback?
    6. Constructive criticism is almost always welcome!
  6. What is your favorite thing about your career?
    7. As for tutoring, I love being able to learn and improve my knowledge, as well as having the satisfaction of being able to help people! As for the rest of my career, I am not quite sure yet what it will be, but I imagine it would be the satisfaction of turning ideas and problems into usable code.
  7. Do you consider yourself a pessimist or an optimist?
    8. Interestingly enough, I was a pessimist as a kid, but then I heard that optimists are more successful, so I made a conscious effort to become an optimist!
  8. Do you believe in second chances?
    9. Absolutely! Everyone makes mistakes. Once I decide I am fond of someone, there is very little they can do that would make me want to cut contact.
  9. What is your proudest accomplishment?
    10. Tutoring college Calculus as a minor; getting a merit-based, 100% tuition scholarship at Florida Tech; and interning for Royal Caribbean Group! As for hobbies, I have learned some of my favorite songs on the piano, won an art contest, won a beauty pageant, etc. Also, got on the ladder in Pokemon Showdown Gen 9 Random Doubles!
  10. What’s your biggest regret in life?
    11. Not getting an additional BS (in Computer Science) at Florida Tech! Also, not getting an Associate's in Nursing while I dual-enrolled, so I could be a nurse while going to FIT.
  11. What do you like to do that most people don't like to do?
    12. I find repetitive tasks to be quite enjoyable.
  12. What are your thoughts on death?
    13. Honestly, I'm at peace with whenever it is my time to go (or others'). Dying is part of nature and part of life. It's what makes life so special -- being alive forever would not be pretty.
  13. Are you a morning person or a night owl?
    14. I'm a mix between a morning person and an afternoon person.
  14. What do you want to change about yourself?
    15. I wish I was naturally carefree instead of always having to make a conscious effort at it. I've struggled my whole life with OCD (I'm in therapy and on meds now). I finally feel like it's under control, but I'd rather just have never had it in the first place!
  15. What’s one thing that bothers you most about the world today?
    16. Social media. It's toxic and a plague on humanity (in my opinion).
  16. What are your favorite animals and why?
    • Dogs -- they are loving and loyal to the end. Truly woman's best friend!
    • Bottomfeeders (catfish, plecos, etc.) -- they are cute, covered in whiskers, and kinda dumb. XD
    • Shrimp -- they are so fascinating to watch, I love how they glide in the water and use their long whiskers.
    • Bunnies -- they are just so adorable.
  17. What are your top favorite books?
    • The Power of Habit
    • Atomic Habits
    • Happiness is the Way
    • Deep Work
    • Digital Minimalism
    • The Willpower Instinct
    • Your Fully Charged Life
    • By the way, here is my Goodreads profile!
  18. How do you feel about God?
    19. I feel that He loves everyone.
  19. What's on your bucket list?
    20. Getting married, having a moderately nice house with an aquarium (as well as an outdoor pond), and having a few dog children. :) Also, being able to manage my time impeccably. Sometimes, I'd choose having a "boring" day of being productive over a day that's meant to be exciting. I feel good about myself when I accomplish my goals.
  20. What are you most thankful for?
    21. My health, my hobbies, my loved ones, and the opportunities given to me in life!
